broccoli

['brɒkəli], ['brɑːkəli]


Image Search
n. 西兰花；花椰菜；甘蓝花
a cabbage of a variety similar to the cauliflower, bearing heads of green or purplish flower buds. It is widely cultivated as a vegetable.

For dinner, he likes chicken and broccoli.正餐他喜欢吃鸡肉和花椰菜。

broccolini


Image Search
布罗科利尼
a vegetable that is a hybrid of broccoli and kale, with small florets on slender stalks.
In a pot filled with 2 inches of water, steam the broccoli or Broccolini until the stems are just tender, 4 to 5 minutes. Transfer to a platter. 在一锅充满了2升的水，蒸，使青花菜或椰菜直到茎刚变软，4至5分钟。然后放到一个大盘子。

[pə'paɪə], [pə'paɪə]

n. 木瓜
a tropical fruit shaped like an elongated melon, with edible orange flesh and small black seeds.
the fast-growing tree that bears the papaya, native to warm regions of America. It is widely cultivated for its fruit, both for eating and for papain production.
He ate a papaya and his tummy got bigger.他吃了一个木瓜，肚子变大了一些。
