n. 山药；番薯
the edible starchy tuber of a climbing plant, widely distributed in tropical and subtropical countries.

a sweet potato.
The main crops are corn, millet, yam, naked oats, buckwheat, and other small grains.主要农作物为玉米、小米、山药、莜麦、荞麦和其他的小杂粮。

A true yam is a starchy edible root generally imported to the United States from the Caribbean.真正的番薯是指一种可使用的淀粉质根菜类食物，通常从加勒比海输入美国。
