radish

['rædɪʃ], ['rædɪʃ]


Image Search
n. （小）萝卜
a swollen pungent-tasting edible root, especially a variety that is small, spherical, and red, and eaten raw with salad.
the plant of the cabbage family that yields the radish.
She carved a flower out of a radish.她把萝卜刻成一朵花。
Please help me, Pull up the radish.请来帮我，拔萝卜吧。

lettuce

['letɪs], ['letɪs]


Image Search
n. 莴苣；生菜；<俚>纸币
a cultivated plant of the daisy family, with edible leaves that are a usual ingredient of salads. Many varieties of lettuce have been developed with a range of form, texture, and color.
used in names of other plants with edible green leaves, e.g., lamb's lettuce, sea lettuce.
paper money; greenbacks.
She never grew lettuce in her garden.她从不在她的花园里培育莴苣。
I like the delicious salad of crisp lettuce and tomatoes.我喜欢这种脆莴苣和番茄做的沙拉,很好吃。
We need a head of lettuce, a bunch of carrots, and a bag of onions.我们要一棵生菜，一把胡萝卜和一袋洋葱。

leek

[liːk], [liːk]


Image Search
n. 韭葱；青蒜
a plant related to the onion, with flat overlapping leaves forming an elongated cylindrical bulb that together with the leaf bases is eaten as a vegetable. It is used as a Welsh national emblem.
When I am young I grow some leek in a pot.小时候我曾在花盆里种了些韭葱。
Throughout the text, she records recipes for French cookery varying in complexity from leek broth to croissants.这本书记录了各种各样的法国烹调菜谱，从韭葱肉汤到羊角面包。
