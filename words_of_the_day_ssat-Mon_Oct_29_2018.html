androgynous

[æn'drɒdʒənəs], [æn'drɑːdʒənəs]

adj. 雌雄同体的；雌雄同花的；兼有男女两性的
partly male and partly female in appearance; of indeterminate sex.
having the physical characteristics of both sexes; hermaphrodite.
In the last chapter Woolf touched the possibility of an androgynous mind.在最后一章，伍尔夫谈到了所谓“雌雄同体”的想法。
My look is very androgynous, and I consider myself a fashion chameleon.我的外表十分“中性化”，所以我觉得自己能成为时尚界的“变色龙”。
We are less threatened by the opposite sex within us and become more androgynous.我们较不会受到异性的威胁,而变得更中性化。
